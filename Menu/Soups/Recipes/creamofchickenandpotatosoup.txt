Melt the butter in a large pot over medium heat,
Stir in the flour until absorbed thoroughly by the butter,
Add the onion and chives and saute for 5 minutes, Pour in the cream water  milk cumin salt ground black pepper garlic powder thyme potatoes and chicken,
Reduce heat to low and let simmer for 1 hour. Remove from heat let cool slightly pour into individual bowls and garnish each with parsley.
